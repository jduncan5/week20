The Redwood Redesign

For this project, my team and were tasked with choosing a not for profit organization and redesigning a few pages of the organization’s website. We conducted a heuristic analysis of the current site of the organization as well as interviewed industry professionals to get a robust understanding of the purpose of the organization and the needs of its clients to reimagine better user experience (UX), and user interface (UI) designs for the organization. Ultimately, our goal was to ensure better usability and accessibility for the primary clients, which are women and children fleeing abuse, in search if a safe space and new beginning.
